What is a Computer Virus?

A computer virus is a code or program that is capable of affecting your computer data by corrupting or destroying them. Also called malicious software program or "malware".

Viruses can make duplicate copies of itself at a rapid pace. They spread across every folder and damage the data of the computer system.
