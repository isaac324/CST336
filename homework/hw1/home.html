What is a Computer Virus?

A computer virus is a code or program that is capable of affecting
your computer data by corrupting or destroying them. Also called
malicious software program or "malware".Viruses can make duplicate
copies of itself at a rapid pace. They spread across every folder
and damage the data of the computer system.



Where did it come from?

Computer can find their way into your computer through many methods:

  • Downloading files from Internet
  • Using a pen drive
  • Opening an email attachment
